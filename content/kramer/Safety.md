title: Safety

Advocating for yourself improves the game for everyone.

Though a certain type and amount of discomfort is one of many desired outcomes for a conflict-oriented game with horror elements, it needs to be actively managed during play.

## Canned Lines
Below is a set of social eject buttons.  
Each provides a *(hopefully)* easily communicated and understood way to bail out of distasteful content.

### "New Choice"
> This could head in a bad direction, let's steer away.

### "Ouch"
> A boundary has been crossed

### "Time Out"
> Stop the game. Let's debrief.
